# Brand-3: Video Prompts

**Target Models:** Kling 2.0 / Sora
**Brand Direction:** Rugged Authenticity
**Color Grading:** Warm earthy tones, film grain, natural light, documentary feel
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Duration:** 5-10 seconds per clip

---

## Hero Section — Background Video

**Prompt:**
Documentary-style shot of a diver sitting on the edge of a weathered wooden dive boat, putting on his mask and rolling backward into calm tropical water. Natural warm morning light, the boat rocks gently. Earthy color grading with deep greens and warm tan tones. Subtle film grain texture. The camera is handheld, slightly imperfect, like a documentary crew captured a real moment. Tropical harbor with fishing boats in the soft background. Authentic, human, unhurried. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Usage:** Hero section background, looped, muted autoplay

---

## About Section — The Crew

**Prompt:**
Candid documentary shot of a small dive crew on a boat deck sharing coffee at dawn. They sit among diving equipment — tanks leaning against the gunwale, wetsuits draped over rails. One points toward the water, the others look. Natural golden hour light, warm earthy tones, deep green ocean in the background. Gentle boat rocking. Film grain, slightly desaturated, warm. The camera observes from a respectful distance, like a documentary. Real people, real moment.

**Usage:** About section background or inline video

---

## Services Section — Underwater Welding

**Prompt:**
Documentary-style underwater shot of a diver welding. The warm orange glow of the welding arc illuminates sediment and bubbles in murky tropical water. The diver works steadily, experienced hands guiding the tool. Natural underwater lighting mixed with the warm arc glow. Earthy warm color grading, film grain, slightly soft focus on the edges. Not glamorized — real working conditions. The camera drifts slightly, handheld, authentic.

**Usage:** Services section, plays on scroll

---

## Services Section — Prep and Teamwork

**Prompt:**
A diver stands waist-deep at the stern of a work boat as crewmates hand down equipment from the deck above. Ropes, tools, a heavy bag lowered by hand. Tropical harbor, late afternoon light cutting warm and low. The crew works in practiced rhythm without speaking. Documentary camera, natural color, warm earthy greens and tans. Film grain. A moment of teamwork captured candidly — the kind of thing that happens every day but rarely gets filmed.

**Usage:** Services section, plays on scroll

---

## CTA Section — Boat in Harbor

**Prompt:**
Wide shot of a traditional wooden dive boat moored in a calm tropical harbor at late afternoon. Warm golden light, turquoise shallow water, small village structures along the shore. A figure walks along the boat deck, coiling rope. Other boats bob gently nearby. The camera slowly drifts laterally at water level. Warm earthy color grading, film grain, natural sound atmosphere. Peaceful, grounded, local. A place where real work happens.

**Usage:** CTA section background, looped

---

## Page Transition — Breaking Surface

**Prompt:**
Underwater looking up as a diver breaks the surface from below. The camera is submerged, capturing the moment legs kick upward through green-teal water into warm sunlight above. The surface ripples and refracts golden light. As the diver breaks through, the image floods with warm natural light. Film grain, earthy tones, the shift from underwater quiet to surface warmth. A feeling of returning, of surfacing from real work.

**Usage:** Page transition element or loading screen
