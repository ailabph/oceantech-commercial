## 3. About Section Copy

**Section Headline:**
New company. Old hands.

**Paragraph 1:**
Oceantech Commercial Diving was established in 2016, but our story starts much earlier. Our founders have been in the water since 1981 — first as recreational divers working Cebu's reefs, then as underwater professionals serving the maritime and construction industries across the Philippines. That's over 45 years of reading currents, solving problems at depth, and bringing people home safe. In fact, the family behind Oceantech — the owners of Crispina Aquatics — were the first PADI-certified diving instructors in the Philippines, a distinction that speaks to the depth of our roots in this industry.

**Paragraph 2:**
When we formed Oceantech, the goal was straightforward: take everything we'd learned in those years and build a commercial diving company that does things the right way. No shortcuts on safety. No guesswork on execution. Just experienced divers, proper equipment, and honest work — the kind of outfit we'd want to hire if we were on the other side of the table.

**Paragraph 3:**
Based in Maribago, Lapu-Lapu City, we're proud to operate from the heart of the Central Visayas. Cebu's maritime heritage runs deep, and so does ours. We know these waters, we know this industry, and we show up ready to work.

## 4. Services Section Copy

**Section Headline:**
What we do below the surface.

**Intro Paragraph:**
We handle the full range of commercial diving work — from routine hull cleaning to complex underwater welding and structural repair. Every job gets the same level of preparation, the same safety standards, and the same crew discipline. Here's what we bring to the table.

---

### Underwater Welding
**Reliable welds in demanding conditions.**
Our certified divers perform wet and habitat welding on hulls, pipelines, and submerged structures. We work to code, in current, and on your schedule — because downtime costs money.

### Hull Cleaning
**Keeping your vessel efficient and seaworthy.**
Marine growth slows you down and drives up fuel costs. We provide thorough hull cleaning services that restore hydrodynamic performance and extend the life of your coatings, with minimal disruption to your operations.

### Pier & Wharf Cleanup
**Maintaining the structures your operations depend on.**
Piers, wharves, and port infrastructure take a beating from the sea. We handle debris removal, marine growth clearing, and underwater structural assessments to keep your waterfront facilities in working order.

### Ship & Vessel Maintenance
**Keeping fleets operational without dry dock delays.**
Not every repair needs a dry dock. Our divers perform propeller polishing, anode replacement, valve servicing, and general underwater maintenance — so your vessels stay in service longer between scheduled haul-outs.

### Underwater Cutting
**Precision cutting where access is limited to divers.**
We perform oxy-arc and thermic cutting on submerged steel, concrete, and mixed structures. Whether it's salvage work, decommissioning, or clearing obstructions, our crew handles it safely and efficiently.

### Survey & NDT Inspection
**Clear reporting on what's happening below the waterline.**
We conduct underwater surveys and non-destructive testing — including ultrasonic thickness measurements, visual inspections, and CCTV documentation — to give you an accurate picture of structural condition. Our reports are detailed, honest, and built for decision-making.

## 5. Process Section Copy

**Section Headline:**
How we work.

### Step 1 — Project Brief
You tell us what you need. We listen, ask the right questions, and scope the job. Whether it's a quick hull inspection or a multi-phase repair, every project starts with a clear understanding of your requirements, timeline, and constraints.

### Step 2 — Site Assessment
Before anyone goes in the water, we evaluate the site. Water conditions, structural context, access points, safety considerations — we plan the dive and dive the plan. No surprises.

### Step 3 — Execution
Our crew mobilizes with the right equipment and the right people for the job. Work is carried out to specification, with constant communication between the dive team and surface support. Safety protocols are followed on every dive, every time.

### Step 4 — Reporting
When the work is done, you get a complete report — photos, video, measurements, and findings. We document everything so you have a clear record for your files, your class society, or your next maintenance decision.
