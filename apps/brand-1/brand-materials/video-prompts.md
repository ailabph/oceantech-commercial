# Brand-1: Video Prompts

**Target Models:** Kling 2.0 / Sora
**Brand Direction:** Heritage Warmth
**Color Grading:** Warm teal and orange tones, natural tropical light, golden hour
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Duration:** 5-10 seconds per clip

---

## Hero Section — Background Video

**Prompt:**
Slow cinematic shot of a commercial diver descending into clear tropical ocean water, sunlight rays penetrating from the surface creating warm teal and golden light beams. The diver wears full surface-supplied diving equipment and moves deliberately downward. Warm color grading with deep teal water and orange-gold sunlight. Gentle bubbles rise past the camera. Professional underwater cinematography, smooth steady movement, natural tropical ocean environment. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Usage:** Hero section background, looped, muted autoplay

---

## About Section — Heritage Reel

**Prompt:**
Slow pan across a weathered wooden dive boat at sunrise in a tropical harbor. Morning golden light illuminates diving equipment laid out on the deck — tanks, regulators, masks, wetsuits. The camera moves slowly from left to right, revealing the calm teal ocean and distant green islands behind. Warm, nostalgic color grading with soft lens flare. A sense of quiet preparation before the day's work begins. Documentary feel, steady cinematic movement.

**Usage:** About section background or inline video element

---

## Services Section — Underwater Welding

**Prompt:**
Close-up of underwater welding in progress. A commercial diver in a surface-supplied helmet steadily welds a joint on a metal structure beneath the surface. Bright orange welding arc illuminates the surrounding dark teal water, sparks and small bubbles drifting upward. The camera slowly pushes in toward the work. Warm teal and orange color palette, cinematic depth of field, professional underwater footage feel.

**Usage:** Services section, plays on scroll or hover

---

## Services Section — Hull Cleaning

**Prompt:**
A commercial diver works alongside the hull of a large vessel underwater, using a cleaning tool that sends clouds of marine growth drifting away. The massive dark hull stretches into the distance. Natural light filters down from above in warm teal tones. The camera slowly tilts upward along the hull, revealing the scale. Steady, professional, documentary feel.

**Usage:** Services section, plays on scroll or hover

---

## CTA Section — Boat at Golden Hour

**Prompt:**
Aerial drone shot slowly circling a commercial diving support vessel anchored in calm tropical waters at golden hour. Warm sunset light reflects off the water surface in orange and gold tones. The teal ocean gradually deepens in color toward the edges. Small crew members visible on deck preparing equipment. Smooth, slow orbital camera movement, cinematic color grading, peaceful but purposeful atmosphere.

**Usage:** CTA section background, looped

---

## Page Transition — Diver Surfacing

**Prompt:**
Underwater shot looking upward as a commercial diver slowly ascends toward the surface. Sunlight creates a bright warm glow at the surface with light rays radiating downward through teal water. The diver rises steadily, silhouetted against the light. Bubbles trail upward. Slow motion, warm teal and gold color grading, sense of emergence and completion.

**Usage:** Page transition element or loading screen
