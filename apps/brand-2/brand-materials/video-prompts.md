# Brand-2: Video Prompts

**Target Models:** Kling 2.0 / Sora
**Brand Direction:** Industrial Precision
**Color Grading:** Dark navy, high contrast, gold/warm highlights against deep shadows
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Duration:** 5-10 seconds per clip

---

## Hero Section — Background Video

**Prompt:**
Dramatic slow-motion shot of a commercial diver walking along the ocean floor, illuminated by a single powerful work light cutting through dark water. Deep navy blue darkness surrounds the diver, with the golden work light creating stark dramatic shadows on the seabed. The diver moves with deliberate authority, equipment heavy and industrial. High contrast cinematic lighting, dark and powerful atmosphere, professional commercial diving footage. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Usage:** Hero section background, looped, muted autoplay

---

## About Section — Equipment Reveal

**Prompt:**
Slow tracking shot across heavy commercial diving equipment arranged on a steel deck. The camera glides at low angle over welding rigs, surface-supplied helmets, hydraulic tools, and thick umbilical cables. Hard industrial lighting from above creates sharp highlights on metal surfaces against deep shadows. Dark moody atmosphere with warm golden accent light reflecting off polished equipment. Precise, deliberate camera movement, no shaking. Dark navy and gold color grading.

**Usage:** About section background or inline video

---

## Services Section — Underwater Welding

**Prompt:**
Extreme close-up of an underwater welding arc. Intense golden-white light erupts from the welding point, sending sparks streaming through dark water. The camera holds steady on the arc, showing molten metal meeting steel in the deep. Dark navy water surrounding the brilliant point of light. Sparks drift and fade into darkness. High contrast, dramatic, powerful. Slow motion. The precision of industrial work captured in cinematic detail.

**Usage:** Services section, plays on scroll

---

## Services Section — Hull Work

**Prompt:**
Dramatic low-angle shot of a commercial diver ascending along the massive hull of a cargo vessel underwater. The hull stretches endlessly upward and into the distance, dwarfing the diver. A work light on the diver's helmet sweeps across riveted steel plates. Dark navy water, metallic silver hull reflections, single point of golden light. Scale and power. Slow upward camera tilt following the diver. Cinematic, industrial, awe-inspiring.

**Usage:** Services section, plays on scroll

---

## CTA Section — Fleet at Twilight

**Prompt:**
Aerial shot of a commercial vessel and support boat in formation on dark ocean water at twilight. The last golden light of sunset reflects in a narrow band across the water surface. Dark navy sky and ocean dominate the frame. Ship running lights glow warm. Slow steady drone pullback reveals the scale of the vessels against the vast dark ocean. Commanding, powerful, premium atmosphere. Cinematic color grading in deep navy and gold.

**Usage:** CTA section background, looped

---

## Page Transition — Descent

**Prompt:**
First-person perspective descending rapidly into dark ocean water. Surface light fades above as the camera plunges downward. The water transitions from blue to deep navy to near-black. A work light clicks on, illuminating particles in the water. Sharp, dramatic, fast descent with sudden controlled stop. Dark navy with single gold light source. Industrial precision. Power and control.

**Usage:** Page transition element or loading screen
