## 4. Services Section Copy

**Section Headline:**
Critical services. Executed with precision.

**Intro:**
Every service we deliver operates under the same standard: plan it, brief it, execute it, document it. No ambiguity. No improvisation. Below is what we do — and what we've built a reputation doing.

---

### Underwater Welding
**Structural integrity delivered at depth.**
Our certified welders perform wet and habitat welding on hulls, pipelines, offshore structures, and submerged steel — coded to international standards. Weld quality is verified, documented, and built to outlast the environment it operates in.

### Hull Cleaning
**Performance recovery, executed in-water.**
Marine fouling degrades speed, increases fuel consumption, and accelerates coating failure. We remove it — methodically, completely, and with minimal impact to vessel scheduling. Your hull is returned to spec without a dry dock visit.

### Pier & Wharf Cleanup
**Maintaining the infrastructure the industry depends on.**
Port structures deteriorate silently. We perform debris removal, biofouling clearance, and underwater structural assessments on piers, wharves, and jetties — identifying degradation before it becomes a liability and clearing it before it becomes an obstruction.

### Ship & Vessel Maintenance
**Operational continuity without dry dock dependency.**
Propeller polishing, anode replacement, sea chest clearing, valve servicing, blanking operations. We execute in-water maintenance that keeps your fleet moving — reducing downtime, extending service intervals, and eliminating unnecessary haul-outs.

### Underwater Cutting
**Controlled material removal in confined and submerged environments.**
We perform oxy-arc, thermic lance, and hydraulic cutting on steel, concrete, and composite structures. Salvage, decommissioning, obstruction clearing — each cut is planned, controlled, and executed to specification in zero-visibility and high-current conditions.

### Survey & NDT Inspection
**Data-driven assessment of subsurface assets.**
We conduct non-destructive testing — ultrasonic thickness measurement, cathodic potential surveys, visual and CCTV inspections — and deliver reporting built for engineering review. Accurate measurements. Clear documentation. Decisions backed by evidence, not assumption.

## 5. Process Section Copy

**Section Headline:**
Four phases. Zero ambiguity.

### Phase 1 — Brief
Scope defined. Requirements locked. We establish deliverables, timeline, constraints, and success criteria before a single piece of equipment is mobilized. Every operation starts with alignment — not assumptions.

### Phase 2 — Assessment
Site conditions evaluated. Water environment, structural context, access, hazards, and logistics are surveyed and documented. The dive plan is built from data, reviewed by the team, and approved before execution.

### Phase 3 — Execution
Crew deployed with task-specific equipment and clear operational protocols. Work is performed to specification with continuous surface-to-diver communication, real-time monitoring, and strict adherence to the safety management plan.

### Phase 4 — Reporting
Full documentation delivered — photography, video, measurements, findings, and recommendations. Every report is structured for technical review, regulatory compliance, and informed decision-making. The job isn't done until the paperwork is.

### 1. Safety as Operating Standard
Not a policy poster. A non-negotiable operational framework. Every dive is governed by a safety management plan, every crew member is trained to international standards, and every operation is briefed, monitored, and debriefed. Full stop.

Our divers hold recognized commercial diving certifications. Our welders are coded. Our inspection personnel are qualified in NDT methodologies. Based in Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu — strategically positioned for fast mobilization across the Visayas and the broader Philippine archipelago. Shorter lead times. Lower mobilization costs. A team that can be on-site when you need them, not when it's convenient.

Surface-supplied diving systems, coded welding rigs, calibrated NDT instruments, CCTV and recording systems. Maintained, inspected, and ready. We invest in the tools that make precision possible — because the job demands it.
