# Crispina Aquatics Heritage Research

## Key Findings

- **Established:** 1985 (formally as PADI dive shop)
- **Location:** Costabella Tropical Beach Resort, Buyong/Maribago, Mactan Island, Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu
- **Owner:** Danilo Almaden
- **Costabella Resort opened:** July 16, 1981 (founded by Luis and Merciditas Alvarez)
- **Status:** Pioneer dive shop — "one of the first dive shops in Cebu"
- **PADI accredited:** Since 1985
- **Family business:** Most staff are family members
- **Owners are the first PADI instructors (pioneers) in the Philippines**

## Heritage Date

- All archived sources say **1985** for Crispina Aquatics establishment
- Costabella Resort opened **1981** — diving activities may predate formal shop
- "Est. 1980" from original brief is unverified — needs confirmation with owner
- By 2010, archived site stated team had "20+ years of experience"

## What This Means for Oceantech

- The PADI pioneer claim is extremely powerful — first PADI instructors in the Philippines
- Family business angle adds authenticity
- "Pioneer" status is confirmed by multiple sources
- Commercial diving transition happened in 2016 (separate from recreational)
